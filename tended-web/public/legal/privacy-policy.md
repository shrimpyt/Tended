# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [Current Date]**

Welcome to Tended ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your household information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web and mobile applications (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
When you create a household, we may collect:
- **Contact Data:** Email addresses and names for account creation and team invitations.
- **Household Data:** Information you input regarding household inventory, pantry items, and tasks.

### B. AI Data Processing & Image Uploads
- **Images & Receipts:** You may choose to upload images (such as receipts or pantries) for AI processing to extract inventory items.
- **Processing Flow:** These images are processed using third-party AI services (e.g., OpenAI). The images and extracted data are transmitted securely.
- **Data Retention:** We do not use your household or image data to train our own models, nor do we permit our AI service providers to use your data to train their models.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **To Provide the Service:** Manage your inventory, sync data across household members, and enable smart restocking features.
- **AI Processing:** To parse receipts, predict restock levels, and provide smart suggestions for inventory management.
- **Improvement:** To monitor performance, identify bugs, and improve user experience securely.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

- **Household Members:** Any data added to a household is visible to all invited members of that household.
- **Service Providers:** We share data with trusted third parties (e.g., database hosting via Supabase, AI processing via OpenAI) solely for the purpose of operating the Service.
- **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including secure protocols and encrypted databases, to protect your household data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your household data at any time through the application. If you delete your account, your data will be securely removed from our active databases.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mytended.app.
